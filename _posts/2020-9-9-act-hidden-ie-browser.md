<h1>Hidden Browsers</h1>

Question:  What should you use to click on a hidden IE browser?

 - [X] &nbsp;  SimulateClick
 - [ ] &nbsp;  Anchor
 - [ ] &nbsp;  Selector
 - [ ] &nbsp;  Image

## Answer

Option A is correct.

If there is a hidden element then a simulate click will be able to get you access to the hidden IE.
