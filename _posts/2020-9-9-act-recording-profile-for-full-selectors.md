<h1>UiPath selectors and recording profiles</h1>

Question:  Which recording profile generates full selectors?

 - [X] &nbsp;  Basic Recording
 - [ ] &nbsp;  Recording by steps
 - [ ] &nbsp;  Citrix recording

## Answer
